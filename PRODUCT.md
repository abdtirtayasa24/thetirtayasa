# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, clients, startup founders, and collaborators evaluating Abdul F. Tirtayasa's data analytics, automation, and AI enablement work. They need fast evidence of credibility, business context, public project work, contact paths, and safe answers from Tirtayasa AI.

## Product Purpose

The portfolio presents Abdul as a Data Analyst & AI Enabler. It turns version-controlled public content into portfolio pages and grounded AI context, while keeping draft/private material out of both rendering and retrieval. Success means visitors quickly understand Abdul's value, inspect relevant projects, and know how to contact him.

## Brand Personality

Calm, technical, precise. The experience should feel production-ready and trustworthy rather than flashy, cyberpunk, or template-like.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page tropes, excessive neon/cyberpunk effects, decorative gradients, heavy glassmorphism, hero metric clichés, repeated card grids without purpose, and technical decoration that does not improve understanding.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with verified evidence, not inflated claims.
2. Make technical depth readable for business and hiring audiences.
3. Keep the interface restrained: one dark technical system, one green accent, clear hierarchy.
4. Preserve public usability when the backend or AI assistant is unavailable.
5. Treat accessibility, privacy, and credibility as part of the brand.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible contrast, semantic controls, visible focus states, keyboard-operable navigation/chat, readable line lengths, and reduced-motion-safe animation. Do not rely on color alone for state or hierarchy.
